I am an Assistant Professor at [Chonnam National University](https://global.jnu.ac.kr/jnumain_en.aspx), Korea. I am affiliated with [Max Planck Institute for Human Development (MPIB)](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/en), Germany as an Associate Research Scientist. My research focuses on understanding society through data science and complex systems methods, with particular interests in <b>urban computing</b>, <b>social impact of AI</b>, <b>future of work</b>, <b>human mobility</b>, and <b>science of science</b>.
